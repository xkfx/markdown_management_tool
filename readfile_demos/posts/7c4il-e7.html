How do I stop overthinking at night?
 
Kshitiz Adhikari, works at Students
Answered Aug 31
 

First rule is stop thinking about future and living in present. We think and we over think and build the threads of thought but one thing is for sure life is not going to be the how we exactly think it will. It's better to focus on present, just make small goals like daily goals of doing something productive and wasting less time doing nothing.

Whenever the threads of thoughts starts to come, you should realise that it's just the thinking. Don’t let your mind build threads of thoughts that originated from nowhere.

Planning and working out on your plan helps but overthink increases the stress.
